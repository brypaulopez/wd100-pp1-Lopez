Nueno Avenue

The Nueno family is closely associated with the founding of Imus. Records indicate that a certain Agustin Nueno was one of the founders of the town in the late 18th century, during the Spanish colonial period.

Today, the Nueno family's legacy lives on in Imus City, with their name commemorated in streets, landmarks, and institutions throughout the town.
